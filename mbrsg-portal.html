Announcements
IT, HR and general updates from MBRSG
ITJune 2025
Infraon IT Ticketing System — Now Live
All IT support requests should now be submitted through the new Infraon ticketing system at mbrsg.infraon.app. This replaces email-only requests and allows real-time tracking of your ticket status. For urgent issues, you may still call or email the IT team directly.
ITMay 2025
CrowdStrike Falcon EDR — Mandatory on All Devices
CrowdStrike Falcon endpoint protection is now deployed on all MBRSG staff devices. Do not disable or uninstall this software. If you experience any performance issues related to CrowdStrike, contact the IT helpdesk immediately. This is a mandatory security requirement.
ITMay 2025
Letsignit Email Signature — Action Required
All staff email signatures are now managed centrally via Letsignit. If your email signature is incorrect or missing, please contact the IT helpdesk at ithelpdesk@mbrsg.ac.ae with your correct name, title and department. Do not manually edit your email signature in Outlook.
HRJune 2025
Leave Applications — Smart Employee App
Reminder: All leave requests (annual, sick, emergency) must be submitted through the Smart Employee App. Leave requests must be approved by your direct manager before the leave date. For leave balance enquiries or policy questions, contact Sara Bin Jarsh (HR Manager) at sara.binjarsh@mbrsg.ac.ae.
HRJune 2025
Eid Al Adha Holiday Notice
The official Eid Al Adha holiday dates will be announced by the UAE government. Employees are requested to submit any planned leave requests through the Smart Employee App at least 5 working days in advance. Contact HR for further details regarding holiday entitlements.
HRApril 2025
New Employee Onboarding Process Update
HR will now provide IT with a standardized Excel onboarding form for all new hires including: employee code, name, designation, department, email, office location, and joining date. IT will complete system setup within 2 business days of receiving the completed form. Contact hanan.darwish@mbrsg.ac.ae for queries.
GeneralJune 2025
MBRSG AI Portal — Now Available
The MBRSG AI Employee Portal is now live. This tool provides AI-powered assistance for drafting emails, understanding MBRSG procedures, document generation, and more. Built specifically for MBRSG staff with full knowledge of our systems and processes. For feedback or issues, contact the IT team.
GeneralMay 2025
GRP System — Attachment File Name Policy
When uploading attachments to GRP (purchase requisitions, expense claims), file names must not contain special characters (/, \\, *, ?, #, etc.) or Arabic text. Rename files to use only English letters, numbers, and hyphens/underscores before uploading. Files with invalid names will cause submission errors.
